NetShapes' Philosophy

NET SHAPES, INC. is a leading investment casting foundry dedicated to providing superior value through our commitment to a higher standard of quality. Our quality driven philosophy positions us to supply our customers' need for castings in a wide variety of alloys with complex configurations, precise dimensional tolerances, high volume quantities, short lead times, and competitive prices. NET SHAPES' responsive customer support is designed to meet the timely needs of every customer. You gain the reliability and expertise of our investment casting foundry team who is devoted to exceeding the demands of our aerospace, military and commercial customers. We welcome the opportunity to introduce ideas that may produce considerable cost savings by reducing machine time, refining surface finishes, and improving reproducibility in accordance with your unique casting requirements.
